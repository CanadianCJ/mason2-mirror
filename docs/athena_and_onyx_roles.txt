Roles of Athena and Onyx
========================

Athena:
- Acts as a dashboard and intelligence layer for Mason2.
- Aggregates system health data, tracks important files, and provides summaries of system status.
- Helps Mason2 maintain awareness of the state of Onyx and other components.

Onyx:
- A core application or service managed by Mason2.
- Subject to health checks and performance monitoring.
- Provides critical functionality that Mason2 and Athena depend on.

Both Athena and Onyx are essential to the overall operation and health of the Mason2 ecosystem. Mason2's role is to monitor, coordinate, and propose improvements for both.
